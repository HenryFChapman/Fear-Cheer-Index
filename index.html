üìä Economic Share of Voice

The Economic Share of Voice (ESoV) provides a normalized view of economic discussions across US-based social media and online platforms over the past two months. By excluding premium news sources, we focus on capturing the authentic voice of everyday consumers and businesses.

Our analysis leverages Infegy's AI Assist to identify and track economic-related topics, ensuring comprehensive coverage of relevant discussions. This approach allows us to monitor whether people are talking more or less about economic issues in recent weeks, providing valuable insights into public engagement with economic topics.

The ESoV metric is particularly valuable as it helps identify shifts in public attention to economic matters, which can serve as an early indicator of changing economic sentiment and potential market movements.

Methodology

The Fear-Cheer Index is calculated using advanced sentiment analysis of social media and news data. Our methodology combines multiple metrics to provide a comprehensive view of economic sentiment.

Data Collection

We analyze millions of social media posts and news articles daily, focusing on economic and financial discussions. Our data sources include major social media platforms, news outlets, and financial forums.

Sentiment Analysis

Using natural language processing, we classify each mention as positive, negative, or neutral. The analysis considers context, tone, and economic implications of the content.

Metric Calculation

Each metric is calculated using weighted sentiment scores, normalized against total volume. This ensures consistent measurement across different time periods and topics.

Index Composition

The Fear-Cheer Index combines multiple sentiment indicators, each weighted based on their historical correlation with economic outcomes and market movements.
